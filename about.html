Who we are ?

GoMyCode was founded in 2017 on the premises of our passion for digital technology and
the realization that the gap between the classical training offered and the job offers
available needed to be bridged.
We have built a real digital community and we are proud to be pioneers in Africa in this area.
We opened our doors in Algeria in 2020 with the vision of expanding the GoMyCode community and
sharing our passion for digital with the entire MENA region.
